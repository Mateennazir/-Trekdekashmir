Overview

If you’re an adventure lover and fascinated with the nature’s beauty, then Kashmir must be the next destination in your travel list. Kashmir is the valley of nature’s magnificent beauty resting at the lap of the north ranges of Himalaya. To witness the beauty of this place, plan a trip to Kashmir once in your lifetime. To experience the true experience of Kashmir sightseeing plan a trip with us. Our representative will accommodate you upon your arrival at Srinagar and will guide you to experience 8 best Kashmir Day tours.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam, often referred to as the "Valley of Shepherds," is a picturesque town in the Anantnag district of Jammu and Kashmir. It's situated on the banks of the Lidder River and surrounded by lush greenery.

Camping Experience:

Pahalgam is a popular destination for camping enthusiasts. The serene environs and breathtaking landscapes make it an ideal spot for camping. Campsites here offer a chance to experience the tranquility of nature while being close to adventure activities like trekking, horse riding, and river rafting. You can choose from a range of campsites, from basic to luxury, depending on your preferences.

Best Time to Camp:

The best time for camping in Pahalgam is during the summer months from May to September when the weather is pleasant and conducive for outdoor activities.

Sonamarg

Sonamarg, which translates to "Meadow of Gold," is a mesmerizing valley town in the Ganderbal district of Jammu and Kashmir. It's renowned for its alpine meadows, pristine lakes, and snow-capped peaks.

Camping Experience:

Sonamarg offers a unique camping experience surrounded by the majestic Himalayan mountains. Campers can wake up to the sight of glaciers and crystal-clear streams. The camping sites are usually set up in proximity to famous trekking trails, making it an excellent base for trekkers and adventure seekers.

Doodhpathri

Doodhpathri, known as the "Valley of Milk," is a hidden gem nestled in the Budgam district of Jammu and Kashmir. It's characterized by rolling meadows, dense forests, and the glistening Doodhganga River.

Camping Experience:

Camping in Doodhpathri offers a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the unspoiled beauty of nature. The campsites are usually set amid lush green landscapes, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills. It's a tranquil escape for those seeking solitude and serenity.

Best Time to Camp:

Doodhpathri is best visited during the summer months from May to July when the meadows are covered in vibrant wildflowers, and the weather is ideal for camping.
