Welcome to TrekDekashmir

Trek de Kashmir is one of the best travel agencies located in Kashmir to help you travel better on your next leisure vacation or business trip. Our first-hand travel experience and relationships with airlines, car rentals, hotels and resorts assure you an exceptional and personalized travel experience thus creating a lifetime of memories.
